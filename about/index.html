About

The first version of this site was created in 2010 using Django and sheer happiness at remembering how fun web development can be, with the right tools. In 2018 I migrated it to Pelican.

I'm a software developer, based in Ireland. I care a lot about free and open-source software, and am usually interested in the crossing of education and technology. I tremendously enjoy sharing what I know and teach workshops from time to time.
